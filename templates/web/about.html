About usWho we are

Our Vision

At Heartima, we envision ourselves as a leading provider of high-quality, homemade pickles and condiments, crafted using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients. We are committed to offering a variety of delicious and authentic pickles, using mango, lemon, chillies, dates, and elephant yam, all sourced locally from Kerala, India. Our passion lies in preserving culinary heritage and bringing the taste of homemade goodness to every table. We strive to become a trusted brand synonymous with quality, freshness, and the unique flavors of Kerala.

Our Mission

Heartima's mission is to create a taste of tradition in every jar. We are dedicated to using time-tested recipes and locally sourced ingredients from Kerala to produce delicious, all-natural pickles and condiments. We believe in empowering home cooks and small farms by providing a platform for their skills and produce. Through sustainable practices and responsible sourcing,
we aim to bring the bounty of Kerala to your table while preserving our culinary heritage for generations to come.

Who We Are

Heartima is a family-run business based in Karipur, Kerala, India. Our story began with a love for homemade pickles and a desire to share the unique flavors of Kerala with the world. We use generations-old recipes passed down through our family, featuring fresh, locally-sourced ingredients like mango, lemon, chilies, dates, and elephant yam. At Heartima, we believe in preserving tradition and creating authentic, high-quality pickles that bring a taste of home to every table.
